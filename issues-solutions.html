Featured Resources

The Holy Quran - Birmingham Manuscript

Dated 568-645 CE - University of Birmingham

One of the oldest Quran manuscripts in existence, carbon-dated to the time of Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him). This manuscript perfectly matches the modern Quranic text.

Codex Sinaiticus - Oldest Complete Bible

4th Century CE - British Library

The oldest complete copy of the Christian Bible in Greek, containing significant textual variations from modern Bibles. Essential for biblical textual criticism.

Dead Sea Scrolls - Complete Collection

3rd Century BCE - 1st Century CE

Ancient Jewish religious manuscripts discovered near the Dead Sea. Includes the oldest known copies of the Hebrew Bible and provides crucial insights into Jewish history and biblical transmission.
