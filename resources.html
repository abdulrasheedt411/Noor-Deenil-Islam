Comparative Religious Studies

Prophethood in Abrahamic Faiths

  • Muhammad in Bible: Deuteronomy 18:18, John 14:16, Song of Songs 5:16 Christian & Jewish Scriptures
  • Continuity of Message: Same core message from Adam to Muhammad Quran 42:13, 3:84
  • Seal of Prophets: Finality of Prophethood in Islam Quran 33:40
  • Historical Verification: Multiple independent accounts of Prophet's life Byzantine, Syriac, Armenian sources

Scriptural Preservation Comparison

  • Quran Preservation: Oral & written transmission, mass memorization 7th century manuscripts still exist
  • Bible Transmission: Multiple versions, translation variations No original manuscripts exist
  • Torah Preservation: Masoretic text standardization Dead Sea Scrolls show variations
  • Historical Manuscripts: Early Quran manuscripts match modern text Birmingham Quran manuscript (568-645 CE)

Theological Concepts Comparison

  • Concept of God: Pure Monotheism vs Trinity vs Complex Unity Quran 112, Deuteronomy 6:4, John 1:1
  • Divine Nature: Transcendent vs Incarnate Quran 42:11, John 1:14
  • Salvation: Faith & Works vs Faith Alone vs Covenant Quran 103:1-3, Ephesians 2:8-9, Exodus 19:5
  • Afterlife: Detailed description vs vague references Quran vs Bible comparative analysis

Historical & Archaeological Evidence

  • Quranic Accuracy: Historical details verified by archaeology Pharaoh's body, People of 'Ad, etc.
  • Biblical Archaeology: Contradictions with archaeological findings Exodus timeline issues, Jericho walls
  • Early Islamic History: Documented by Muslims and non-Muslims 7th century non-Muslim accounts
  • Scientific Foreknowledge: Modern science confirming Quranic statements Embryology, water cycle, universe expansion
