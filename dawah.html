African Traditional Religion (ATR)

Special Focus: Africa has over 100 million followers of Traditional Religions. Understanding ATR is crucial for effective dawah in Africa. This module provides deep insights into African spirituality and shows how Islam fulfills and elevates traditional African values.

Ancestral Veneration

Understanding the role of ancestors in ATR and how Islamic concept of righteous predecessors provides a balanced alternative without shirk.

Nature Spirits & Deities

Teaching Methodology

Quranic Method of Dialogue

  • Surah An-Nahl 16:125: "Invite to the way of your Lord with wisdom and good instruction, and argue with them in a way that is best..." The Foundation of Islamic Propagation
